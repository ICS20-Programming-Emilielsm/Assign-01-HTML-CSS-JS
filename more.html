There are many marine animals in the ocean. On this web page this is going to list the 3 coolest in the ocean!

First is the Feather Star

The Feather Star uses its feathers in order to move around the ocean!

Second is the Mantis Shrimp

Mantis Shrimp are little sea crustaceans. They have glowing green backs, red legs, bulging eyes, and long antennas making the Mantis Shrimp one of the most alien-looking creatures!

Third is the Glaucus Atlanticus

The Glaucus Atlanticus, also known as the Blue Dragon, is a type of stinging sea slug. They don't really swim, they just float upside down on the surface of the waves.
